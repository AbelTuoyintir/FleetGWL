# User Manual: Live Vehicle Tracking System

Welcome to the Ghana Water Limited Live Vehicle Tracking System. This manual will guide you through the features and usage of our professional fleet monitoring dashboard.

---

## 1. Administrator Guide

### Overview of the Command Center
The Command Center is your primary interface for real-time fleet visibility.

#### A. The Map Interface
The central area displays all active vehicles as car-shaped icons.
- **Rotation:** Icons rotate based on the actual direction of the vehicle.
- **Color Coding:**
    - **Blue:** Vehicle is currently on an active trip.
    - **Green:** Vehicle is available/idling.

#### B. Map Controls
- **Theme Switcher (Top Right):**
    - **Light:** Best for general daytime use.
    - **Dark:** Ideal for night monitoring or low-glare environments.
    - **Satellite:** View vehicles against real-world terrain and structures.
- **Sync Button:** Force an immediate refresh of all vehicle data.

#### C. Searching and Filtering
Use the **Left Sidebar** to find specific units:
- **Search Bar:** Enter a registration number (e.g., GW-101-24) or a driver's name.
- **Status Filter:** Narrow your view to only "Active" or "In Shop" vehicles.

#### D. Monitoring a Specific Vehicle
1. Click on a vehicle in the sidebar or directly on its map icon.
2. The map will **Fly-to** the vehicle and open a **Detail Card**.
3. **Detail Card Features:**
    - View live Speed (km/h) and status.
    - **Follow Mode:** Click "Follow" to have the camera lock onto the vehicle as it moves.
    - **History Playback:** Click "History" to visualize the path taken in the last 24 hours.

## 2. Driver Guide

### Status Management
As a driver, your visibility on the admin map is linked to your online status.

- **Going Online:** When you log into the system, your status is automatically set to "Online."
- **Availability:** Ensure your assigned vehicle is updated. When you start a task, your vehicle icon will change to Blue (On Trip) on the Admin dashboard.
- **Real-Time Updates:** Your position is automatically updated every 5 seconds while you are active.

## 4. Troubleshooting
- **Map not loading:** Ensure you have an active internet connection.
- **Vehicle seems stuck:** Check the "Last Update" timestamp on the Detail Card. If it's more than 5 minutes old, the driver may be in a poor signal area.
- **Markers jumping:** The markers are designed to move smoothly. If they appear to jump, check if your browser performance is throttled.
